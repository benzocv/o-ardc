About Expert Lecture
In order to offer our students latest insights into the emerging technologies and advancements, various faculties of Amiraj college of Engineering and technology arrange for renowned experts to come and visit the college to deliver expert talks to our students. These expert talks help our students gain strong foothold in their respective discipline and help them stand out among the rest. We arrange atleast Two expert lectures in each departments.Here you can announce your expert lecture to participents.

ARDC also organize some expert lectures in Amiraj College of Engineering and Technology.
